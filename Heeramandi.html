Heeramandi: The Diamond Bazaar


Season 1

Realease year: 2024

The scheming Mallikajaan rules over an elite house of courtesans — but a new rival
threatens her reign as rebellion brews in British-ruled India.


1. Mallikajaan: The Queen of Heeramandi
As Mallikajaan reigns over her courtesans, rebellion grows on the streets. Alamzeb has her first brush with love. Wali shares key information with Bibbo.

2. Fareedanjaan: The Challenger Returns
Heartbroken by a nawab, Lajjo turns to drink and dance to win back her former lover. Mallikajaan reveals a dark secret as her past catches up with her.

3. Waheedajaan - Scarred for Life
The return of Fareedan to Khwabgah raises tensions as Mallikajaan schemes to cling onto her control. Waheedan makes a bold move of her own.


4. Alamzeb: The Innocent Pawn
As her dance debut approaches, Alam questions her place at Heeramandi. Saima unwittingly earns Mallikajaan's ire and Fareedan strikes back.

5. Tajdar: The Lover’s Dilemma
As Mallikajaan and the courtesans fervently search for Alam, Tajdar comes to her rescue. Meanwhile, an enraged Waheedan has a proposal for Fareedan.

6. Tajdar & Alamzeb: Nation vs. Love
Alam and Tajdar’s romance intensifies as a rebellion is being hatched. Mallikajaan and Fareedan uncover dark and disturbing secrets about each other.


7. Bibbojaan - Long Live the Revolution
Cartwright’s scheme against the courtesans unfolds. Seeking a future with Alam, Tajdar approaches Mallikajaan — but her approval comes at a price.

8. Heeramandi: The Swan Song
The fight for freedom reaches the steps of Heeramandi — for both the courtesans and the country. Will the battle end in victory or result in ruination?
